How the Workflow Works

  • 1. Planner Agent: Deconstructs your research topic into specialized, targeted sub-queries.
  • 2. Researcher Agent: Scours web indices and data sources to gather high-quality, relevant facts.
  • 3. Writer Agent: Compiles, cross-references, and edits the findings into a cohesive, publication-ready markdown report.
