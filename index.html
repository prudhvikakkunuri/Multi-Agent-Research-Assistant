ResearchMind — a multi-agent pipeline that turns any topic into a reviewed research report.

Four specialized AI agents work in sequence — searching, reading, writing, and critiquing — to take a single research topic and turn it into a polished, fact-checked report, orchestrated with LangChain and powered by Mistral AI.

about the project

Why a multi-agent approach?

Most AI research tools rely on a single model trying to do everything at once: search, reason, write, and self-check. That works for simple questions but breaks down on anything that requires depth. ResearchMind splits the work across four dedicated agents, each with a narrow job and a clear handoff to the next, similar to how a small research team would divide labor.

Type in a topic, hit run, and watch the pipeline work stage by stage: gathering sources, extracting the real content, drafting the report, then having a critic agent review and score it before it's handed back to you — all through a simple Streamlit interface, with Mistral AI models reasoning at each stage.
