about

Why a multi-agent approach?

Most AI research tools rely on a single model trying to search, reason, write, and self-check all at once. That works for simple questions but breaks down on anything that requires real depth.

ResearchMind splits the work across four dedicated agents, each with a narrow job and a clear handoff to the next — closer to how a small research team would divide labor than a single overloaded model.
